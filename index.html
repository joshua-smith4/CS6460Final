Installing Software Involves Risk

Installing software from unverified sources can have severe impacts on your computer's security. Risks associated with installing software from unverified publishers include:

  • Allowing access to private and sensitive information
  • Increasing vulnerability to computer viruses and malware
  • Modifying files and settings on your computer
